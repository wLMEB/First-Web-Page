Tennis

Information,history:

Tennis was believed to be invented in 12th century northern France where a ball was struck with the palm of the hand. Later on it was developed to be played with a racket.More information about tennis

How to play and score:


Tennis can be played between two or four people. Each person take turns to serve the ball. The serve must go into the serve box located on the opposite side. If the ball is hit in to the net which is located in the center of the court by one player, the other player gets the point. If the ball landed outside of the singles lines in singles or double lines in doubles by one player, the other player or players get the point. The score is counted by 15. 0 love", "15", "30", "40". If you have 40 to 40 it is called "deuce".
